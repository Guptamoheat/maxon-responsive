Financial Partners

We are dedicated to fostering collaborations that deliver optimal advantages to all stakeholders involved. Through our credit solutions, we've established a seamless payment process where brands receive upfront payment for all their invoices, relieving your dedicated sales team of the burden of collection efforts. This empowerment allows your sales team to focus on their core strengths, driving business growth and sales. This inclusive approach also extends benefits to retailers, granting them the flexibility of a 90-day payment window without incurring any fees or interest charges. Furthermore, retailers can take advantage of discounts on their invoice payments, creating a mutually beneficial scenario for all parties. Our financial partners, including Monedo Financial Services, Arthmate, Indifi, and Mintifi, share our vision of simplifying and enhancing the financial aspects of the distribution process, as we collaboratively strive to establish a more efficient ecosystem for our valued stakeholders.
